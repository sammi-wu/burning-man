History

Burning Man is an annual event that takes place in the Black Rock Desert of northern Nevada. The event typically culminates with the symbolic burning of a large wooden effigy, known as "The Man," which is why it's called Burning Man.
Although Burning Man started as a small gathering on a beach in San Francisco in 1986, it has since grown into a massive cultural event and become a symbol of alternative living, creativity, and radical self-expression.
