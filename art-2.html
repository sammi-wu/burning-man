Art:
Large-Scale Installations

There are three kinds of large-scale installations. First, sculptures made from wood, metal, and more, often climbable or interactive, and lit up at night for a surreal effect. Then, The Man, a massive wooden effigy that changes yearly and is burned in a ritual at week’s end, reflects the festival’s theme. Last, the Temple, which is a reflective space for messages and mementos, burned solemnly on the final night.
